# 📊 MISUO Metal Furniture - Sales Dashboard Analysis

## 🏢 About the Company

MISUO Metal Furniture is a company specialized in manufacturing and selling metal furniture products such as gates, railings, and custom metal structures.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance and build an interactive dashboard to help stakeholders monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Data Visualization

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

* Cleaned and transformed raw data using Power Query
* Handled missing and inconsistent values
* Standardized data formats for analysis

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Sales: 1,291,986
* 📦 Total Orders: 154
* 💵 Average Selling Price: 10,254
* 🧍‍♂️ Individual Customers: 91
* 🏢 Corporate Customers: 35

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔝 Best Selling Product

* The top-performing product contributes significantly to total revenue, indicating strong customer demand.

### 🎨 Best Selling Color

* Red and white are the most preferred colors, suggesting customer preference trends.

### 📅 Sales Trend

* Sales peaked in September and showed a declining trend in the following months.

### 🌍 Top Performing Cities

* Cairo and Giza generated the highest sales among all regions.

### 🚚 Delivery Performance

* 60% of orders were delivered on time
* 38% were late
* 1% were canceled

### 📦 Delivery Issues

* A noticeable percentage of orders were not delivered, highlighting potential operational inefficiencies.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters (Platform, Area, Customer Type)
* Dynamic charts for sales analysis
* KPI summary cards
* Delivery performance visualization

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Improve delivery operations to reduce late shipments
* Focus marketing on best-selling products and colors
* Investigate reasons behind sales decline after September
* Expand in high-performing cities like Cairo and Giza


---

## 📌 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance and helps identify trends, customer behavior, and operational gaps to support better decision-making.
